h)
	Services disabled (masked) with systemctl mask:
		- wpa_supplicant.service: We don't use the WiFi.
		- ModemManager.service: We don't also use 2G, 3G or 4G.
		- avahi-daemon.service: Zero-conf netowork discovery. Why.
		- udisks2.service: Service to automount USBs. We don't need it'.
		- apparmor.service: Service to "protect" the apps. We don't need protection, we need speed.
		- NetworkManager.service: Automatic network manager, we use manual configuration.
	
	User services disabled with systemctl --user mask:
		- pipewire.service: Service to capture sound
		- pulseaudio.service
		- org.bluez.service
		- tracker-miner-xxxx-.service

i)
	Create our own service ->
		touch /etc/systemd/system/my-service.service
		chmod +x /etc/systemd/system/my-service.service
		cat my-service.service
			[Unit]
			Description=My Little Script

			[Service]
			User=root
			WorkingDirectory=/root
			ExecStart=/root/my-script.sh

			[Install]
			WantedBy=multi-user.target
